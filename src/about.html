Our Story.

Founded in the heart of The Gambia, born from a shared belief that no woman should walk the path of cancer alone.

Our Mission

To empower and advocate for all those affected by breast cancer through education and community.

Our Joint Vision

To give every woman hope, knowledge, and support—ensuring no one faces the journey alone.

The Visionaries

Leaders of the Movement

Founder

Amie Corr

Amie Corr is an automotive engineer, an upcoming psychologist, and a dedicated businesswoman. As a founder of Breast Cancer Warriors (BCW), she combines her technical expertise with a passion for women’s health, leading impactful awareness campaigns and empowering young women to break barriers — both in engineering, mental health advocacy, and entrepreneurship.

Co-Founder

Tida Njie

Tida Njie is a young social worker at the GGP, an advocate, and a budding coder. As co‑founder of BCW, she combines her dedication to community work with a fresh, youthful perspective. Through outreach, support groups, and educational campaigns, she helps ensure that awareness, compassion, and practical help reach even the most vulnerable women and families affected by breast cancer.
